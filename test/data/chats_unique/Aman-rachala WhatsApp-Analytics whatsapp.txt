*But as UPI arrived, most of these Brands have reported a steep decline in sales of Toffees*. 

*Hershey’s, one of world’s biggest  Toffee makers said India is one of the worst-hit markets in the post COVID Era hitting their expansion plans*

So what really changed ?
 
*Before UPI, shopkeepers would trade Toffees for small change, stating shortage of Coins*

*These small amounts of change over weeks, would amount to becoming vast sums of Money, which were never returned to customers at all, because Toffees were given to customers, in lieu of small change.*

*With UPI, all of this stopped*

*People paid the Exact Amount using UPI, with no scope for exchanging small change, ultimately eating up the daily sales of Toffees*

*This also created a push towards Digital Payments and Toffee sales went down drastically*

*No Toffee company would have ever imagined that Financial products becoming their Competitors* 

 *Toffees were Coin substitutes and UPI replaced this requirement and drastically changed Customer Behavior*

 *In this modern Era, Products which aren’t really a direct Competition, can potentially replace a Product* !!”

 *Technology can impact any Business*
